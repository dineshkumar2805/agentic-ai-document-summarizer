# Business Use Case: Agentic AI Document Summarizer

## The Reality
Every business creates documents daily.
But most documents are never fully read again.

Important knowledge stays locked inside files.

## Why This Is a Problem
- Manual reading is slow
- Summaries depend on people
- Context is lost
- Decisions are delayed

Chatbots do not solve this.
They require copy-paste and do not store knowledge.

## The Agentic AI Approach
This system works like a digital employee:

1. Any document is uploaded
2. The AI agent reads and understands it
3. Key insights are extracted
4. A structured summary is created
5. Knowledge is stored for reuse

No manual effort.
No context loss.
